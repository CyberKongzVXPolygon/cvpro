Note: VIP can use the platform transfer function. Transfer can only be made between the platform VIP and VIP (the transfer cannot be returned successfully)

  2. Correctly filled in VIP username
  3. Click to confirm to complete the transfer (no payment password is required)

4. How to store value

  1. Click the stored value button
  2. Select the amount to be stored
  3. Click to confirm. Enter (payment password)
  4. You will get the exclusive stored value address of your account that you can transfer from any exchange, or wallet.

5. How to withdraw cash

  1. Click the withdrawal button
  2. Enter the withdrawal USDT address (TRC20\ERC20)
  3. Enter the withdrawal quantity
  4. Click the confirm button and enter the payment password
  5. The withdrawal will be successfully completed and your withdrawal will be deposited within 2 hours
